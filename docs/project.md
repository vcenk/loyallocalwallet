# Project Specification — Local Loyalty Wallet

## 1. Product summary

Build a wallet-first loyalty platform for local small businesses. Businesses create their own reward program, customers enroll by QR code, save a loyalty card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet, and staff use a mobile scanner app to add stamps, redeem rewards, and approve bonus actions.

The long-term differentiator is **AI-powered retention**: the system identifies inactive customers, recommends campaigns, and helps local businesses bring customers back automatically.

## 2. Product vision

Small businesses should be able to run a modern loyalty and comeback-marketing system without buying a complex POS system, hiring a marketer, or forcing customers to download another mobile app.

## 3. Core positioning

**No-app loyalty wallet + simple retention automation for local businesses.**

Alternative copy:

- Apple & Google Wallet rewards for local shops.
- Loyalty cards that bring customers back.
- Scan, save, stamp, return.
- A loyalty wallet and comeback-marketing assistant for cafés, salons, restaurants, and shops.

## 4. MVP goal

Launch a working MVP where:

1. A business owner can sign up and create a branded loyalty card.
2. Customers can enroll through a QR code and add the card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet.
3. Staff can scan customer cards and add stamps.
4. Rewards can be redeemed and tracked.
5. Business owners can see customers, visits, redemptions, and inactive customers.
6. The dashboard can suggest a basic win-back campaign.

## 5. Target market

### Primary launch niches

- Cafés
- Bubble tea shops
- Bakeries
- Barbers
- Nail salons
- Pet groomers
- Food trucks
- Small restaurants
- Dessert shops
- Local gift shops

### Best first niche

Start with cafés, bubble tea shops, bakeries, barbers, and nail salons because they naturally have repeat visits and simple reward rules.

## 6. Main user types

### Business owner/admin

Needs to create programs, manage staff, view customers, send campaigns, and understand which customers are inactive.

### Staff member

Needs to scan fast, add stamps, redeem rewards, and approve bonus stamps. The staff flow must work in under 5 seconds.

### Customer

Needs to join quickly, save the card, see progress, receive updates, and redeem rewards without downloading another app.

## 7. MVP scope

### Included in v1

- Business signup and login
- Business profile setup
- Location setup
- Staff invitation and roles
- Loyalty card designer
- Stamp-based loyalty program
- Points-based structure in schema, but optional in UI
- QR code enrollment
- Customer enrollment page
- Apple Wallet pass generation
- Google Wallet pass generation
- Staff scanner app
- Stamp event logging
- Reward redemption
- Bonus stamp reasons
- Customer list
- Basic analytics
- Inactive customer list
- Manual push/update message
- Stripe subscription
- Audit logs

### Not included in v1

- Customer mobile app
- Full POS integration
- Automatic Instagram/TikTok verification
- Rewarding Google reviews
- Marketplace/discovery app
- Neighborhood network
- White-label agency mode
- Advanced AI design generation
- Gift cards or prepaid balances
- Booking system
- Full e-commerce module

## 8. Differentiation

The product should not be a clone of existing digital stamp-card systems. The differentiator is:

> The system tells small businesses who is likely to disappear, what to send them, and when.

### Differentiating v1 features

- Inactive customer list
- Suggested win-back message
- Bonus stamp action engine
- Industry templates
- Multilingual-ready campaign structure
- Simple staff approval for social/share/referral bonuses

### Future differentiators

- AI campaign autopilot
- Slow-day campaign suggestions
- Churn scoring
- Auto-generated multilingual campaigns
- POS integrations
- Local cross-shop network

## 9. Business model

Subscription SaaS for businesses. No charge to customers.

Suggested starting plans:

- Starter: CAD $19/month
- Growth: CAD $39/month
- Pro: CAD $79/month

A 14-day or 15-day free trial is recommended.

## 10. Compliance note

Do not reward customers for Google reviews. The product can send non-incentivized review requests after a positive moment such as a redemption, but the system should not say “leave a review and get a stamp.”
